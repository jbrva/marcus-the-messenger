The Two Rules

⊕ The Adding Rule
When a smaller numeral comes after a bigger one, add them. Bigger first, smaller after.
VIII = 5 + 1 + 1 + 1 = 8
⊖ The Subtracting Trick
When a smaller numeral comes before a bigger one, subtract the small from the big.
IX = 10 − 1 = 9
Only these six subtraction pairs exist: IV (4) · IX (9) · XL (40) · XC (90) · CD (400) · CM (900)

Decoding a Long Numeral

MCMLXXXIV
Break it into chunks:M · CM · LXXX · IV
M is just M:1000
CM = C before M (subtract):1000 − 100 = 900
LXXX = L + X + X + X (add):50 + 10 + 10 + 10 = 80
IV = I before V (subtract):5 − 1 = 4
Add it all up:1000 + 900 + 80 + 4 = 1984
